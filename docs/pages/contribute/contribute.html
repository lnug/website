Contribute

LNUG is organised by volunteers from the London Node community, you can help out in a number of ways..

On the day

  • Arrive at venue 20 minutes early to help set up chairs
  • Help out newcomers
  • Get everyone seated for 7pm
  • Put chairs away and tidy venue
  • Tidy food & drink areas, put empties in the bins
  • Keep the beer on ice!

Other

  • Give feedback
  • Speak/Suggest a talk
  • Help out with issues on the website

There are always things that need doing, get in contact for more details.
